Global V. Local

*Any Variable declared outside of the block is said to have a global scope *accessible everywhere in the program
*NOT A GOOD PRACTICE

Local Scope: any variables defined inside a block using "let" or "const" will only be available inside that block and not be accessible outside of it.
*If "let" and "const" are not used, the variable has a global scope and will be available outside of the block.

Octal and Hexadecimal #'s
